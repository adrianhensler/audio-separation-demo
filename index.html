How It Works

Generate: minimax/speech-02-turbo for TTS, stackadoc/stable-audio-open-1.0 for sound effects  •  Mix: ffmpeg -filter_complex amix  •  Separate: cjwbw/demucs (htdemucs)  •  All models run on Replicate
